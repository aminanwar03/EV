Key Observations

Cost Savings

  • EVs generally have a higher upfront cost, but the gap is decreasing.
  • Monthly charging costs for EVs are significantly lower than fuel costs for non-EVs.
  • Total cost of ownership for EVs can be more economical over 5 years.

Environmental Concerns

  • EVs produce zero emissions, reducing urban air pollution.
  • Manufacturers are adopting eco-friendly production methods.
  • EVs contribute to lower noise pollution in cities.

Government Incentives

  • Subsidies and tax rebates make EVs more affordable.
  • Investment in charging infrastructure supports EV adoption.
  • Policies favoring climate goals are promoting EV usage.
