AtliQ Motors Recommendations

1. Build More Charging Stations

What to Do: Partner with the government and companies to set up more charging stations.

Why: Having plenty of charging spots makes it easier for people to buy and use electric vehicles (EVs).

2. Promote Eco-Friendly Benefits

What to Do: Run ads that highlight how EVs help the environment.

Why: Many people care about climate change, so showing that AtliQ Motors is eco-friendly can attract more customers.

3. Offer Easy Payment Options

What to Do: Provide flexible financing and trade-in deals for customers.

Why: EVs can be expensive, so making it easier to pay can encourage more people to buy them.
